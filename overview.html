Welcome to Inside Out Hotel

Inside Out Hotel is a premier destination that blends luxury and comfort with a unique architectural design. Located in the vibrant area of Leyte, our hotel offers guests an unparalleled experience, combining modern amenities with personalized service. We pride ourselves on being a safe and inclusive space for all visitors, ensuring that everyone feels welcome and valued.

Our Unique Concept

At Inside Out Hotel, we believe in creating an environment that reflects the beauty of both indoor and outdoor living. Our design philosophy is centered around the idea of bringing the outside in, allowing guests to enjoy nature while experiencing the comforts of a luxury hotel. The hotel's layout features expansive windows, open-air spaces, and lush greenery, providing a serene atmosphere that enhances relaxation and enjoyment.
